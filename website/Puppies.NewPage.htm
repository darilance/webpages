Darilance is a small kennel near Huntly in New Zealand's rural Waikato.

 

We breed only occasionally, always aspiring to further improve our Cavaliers.

Parents of any puppies we have will have been heart tested and eye tested

and certified clear of hereditary diseases. They will also have been DNA tested for

CCS/DE (Curly Coat Syndrome/Dry Eye) & EF (Episodic Falling Syndrome) or be

clear by parentage, therefore we guarantee no affected pups will be produced.

As all our puppies come from carefully thought out breeding plans we tend to keep them

a while to see how they mature. Older puppies are sometimes available to loving homes.
